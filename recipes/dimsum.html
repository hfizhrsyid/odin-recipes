Dimsum

Chicken dimsum

Dimsum is a small Chinese dishes that are traditionally enjoyed in restaurant for bunch. They are usually presented in a small plate from 2 to 4 pieces. There are many variances of the dish and it is becoming more and more popular in many parts of the world.

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 and a half bunch cilantro, chopped
  1. Combine all the ingredients
  2. Fill each of the wrapper with a pork mixture
  3. Make the sauce
